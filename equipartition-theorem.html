brain/Equipartition theorem

The basic principle behind the equipartition theorem is that in Thermodynamic equilibrium, the energy is shared equally among its various forms. More directly, linking this to temperature, it says that at equilibrium, any quadratic form of the internal energy is $\frac{1}{2} k_BT$ per degree of freedom, $k_B$ being the Boltzmann constant.
