brain/Standard deviation

The standard deviation is a measure of how much a set of values varies from the expected value or mean $\mu$.

Discrete case

Given a set of measured values, the standard deviation is

$$ \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N (x_i - \mu)^2}, $$

where $\mu$ is the mean of the set.
