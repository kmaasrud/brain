brain/Kroenecker delta

The Kroenecker delta is a function of two indices that return the corresponding element of the identity matrix:

$$\delta_{ij} \equiv \bigg\{\begin{matrix}1, & i = j \ 0, & i \ne j\end{matrix}$$

In Einstein notation, the Kroenecker delta can be represented in these ways:

$$\delta_{ij} \equiv \delta_j^i \equiv \delta^{ij}$$
