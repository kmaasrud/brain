brain/Neovim

Neovim is an extension of the text editor Vim, implementing modern technologies like - most importantly - asynchrony.

Neovim also aims to clean the codebase and implement saner defaults to increase usability for newer users. It is scriptable with Lua and version 0.5 implements my absolute favorite feature of all time: Built-in LSP support. You really didn't know you needed this until you have it, so please do yourself a favor and read up on the documentation.
